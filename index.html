Rock Riding Grip Master

I've always liked trying new things and when I tried rock climbing for the first time
it was no different. I was at my aunt's house and saw they had a rock wall and
wondered what the point of it was. One of my cousins showed me that you just find
ways to climb up the wall. After I had done it a few times he wanted to time me.
After the first time he timed me that was when I really started to like rock climbing.

When ever I had the opportunity to try rock climbing I always did because my family
didnt visit my aunt very often because of how far she lived from us. But when we did
visit that was the first thing I would want to do. After so many years of rock climbing
I built up some of an ego, and when ever I could I would show off to my friends that I
could climb up the wall faster than any of them could.

Something I've always found interesting is why people use certain types of gear wheth
it's a belt or a harness. A harness goes around your whole body where as the safety belt only
goes around your waist and legs.
